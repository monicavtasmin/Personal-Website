About

Smart Home Automation

Introducing, Captura. Captura is a home automation system that allows customers to control their systems from afar, whether at home or elsewhere. As a result, there's no need for them to interact with the objects physically. They can control the system using this all-in-one package system. For example, our users need to turn off their TV but they’re in the garage. They don’t need to come to the living room to do so! Just grab the phone open the app, and change the TV settings to off.

By definition, Captura means to capture in French. In practice, home automation applies Data Capture and Internet of Things concepts to capture users' data, privacy, and customized actions and notifications. Our motto is to provide real products and services.

Captura's goal is to provide interactive, efficient, and advanced home automation technologies that will make many people's lives simpler.
We are aiming to build loyalty and relationships with our clients by providing a competitive price and reliable customer service.

SWOT Analysis

STRENGTH

  • Portable; no need additional device to change settings, customize systems and notifications, and many other helpful things to help users with their home settings.
  • Usable UI design on mobile app that does not require a convoluted set of processes that requires the manual to be used or online resources.
  • User-focused system; considered user privacy and preferences. We will not capture the data if we didn’t get the permission from our users.

WEAKNESS

  • The price tends to be quite costly; not all people can afford the system
  • At this stage, our product is basically still newly introduced. The branding may not be as powerful as our competitors. As a result, we may still lag behind our competition and require more time to gain user loyalty and increase popularity.

OPPORTUNITIES

  • We have a feature that distinguishes our system from our competitors, and that is the battery feature. Users don't have to put our system hardware using only cables, they can use batteries too so that they can be carried anywhere. The battery will also be shown as a percentage on the application dashboard, allowing users to replace it when it is about to run out. Therefore, Captura has the opportunity to stand out and compete in the market

THREATS

  • There are a lot of home automation systems out there. Therefore, they will be powerful competitors with whom Captura must compete.

Color Scheme

We are using an earth tone colour scheme. Earth tones are colours that are inspired by the elements of the earth and are often present in homes to give a choice, relaxing, and refreshing effect. With an earth tone style, the user of our app would feel more homey and relax.

Icons

The icons will be used for our mobile application. We are using icons from Figma plugin (Icons8). These icons are easy to understand since they're commonly used.
