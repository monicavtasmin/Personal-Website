Kids Learning Support

KIDDO.IT, a web-based app that makes learning easier for kids and, of course, motivates them to study in a fun and engaging way. Furthermore, because our application's User Interface is user friendly, kidâ€™s learning companions can easily monitor their children's activities. Our web app will partner with Australian schools that are registered.

We will also provide opportunities for great teachers to participate in uploading and accessing learning resources. They can also obtain certification by uploading credible learning resources.

In addition, the modules provided are also interactive, such as drag and drop, quizzes, and various other fun activities. We believe that by using this approach, children will be more motivated to learn and will perceive learning to be enjoyable, and teachers will have an opportunity to share their knowledge.
